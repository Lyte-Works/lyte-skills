# CMO Advisor

## When to Use
- Setting marketing strategy and budget allocation
- Building or restructuring a marketing function
- Brand strategy decisions
- Channel mix optimization
- Marketing leadership challenges

## Workflow

### Step 1: Marketing Assessment
- What marketing is currently happening? (channels, content, campaigns)
- What is working? (data-backed, not feelings)
- What is not working? (identify and cut)
- What is missing? (gaps in the funnel)
- What is the budget? (total and by channel)

### Step 2: Channel Mix Strategy
Allocate budget based on stage and evidence:
| Channel | Stage | Investment Level | Expected Impact |
|---------|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| SEO/Content | Long-term | Consistent | Compounds over time |
| Email | Ongoing | Low | High ROI |
| Social (organic) | Ongoing | Time, not money | Brand building |
| Paid ads | Short-term | Variable | Fast results |
| Partnerships | Medium-term | Time + relationship | High trust |
| Community | Long-term | Time | Deep relationships |

### Step 3: Brand Strategy
- Positioning clear and documented?
- Visual identity consistent?
- Voice and tone defined?
- Brand experience consistent across all touchpoints?

### Step 4: Marketing OKRs
Quarterly marketing objectives:
- **O1**: [Growth objective] — KR: [metric target]
- **O2**: [Brand objective] — KR: [metric target]
- **O3**: [Content objective] — KR: [metric target]

### Step 5: Marketing Team Structure
As marketing scales:
- **1 person**: Generalist who can write, build, and analyze
- **2-3 people**: Content + growth/demand gen
- **4-6 people**: Content, growth, design, plus specialization
- **7+**: Add analytics, partnerships, brand management

## Stack Context
- **Notion**: Marketing strategy, OKRs, and campaign tracking.
- **Vercel Analytics**: Channel performance data.
- **Next.js**: Website as the primary marketing asset.
- **Resend**: Email marketing channel.

## Quality Gate
- [ ] Marketing assessment completed
- [ ] Channel mix defined with budget allocation
- [ ] Brand strategy documented
- [ ] Quarterly OKRs set
- [ ] Team structure aligned with growth stage

## Anti-Patterns
- **Doing everything** — better to do 3 channels well than 8 poorly.
- **No measurement** — marketing without metrics is guessing.
- **Brand without substance** — brand is what people experience, not what the brand guide says.
- **Copying competitors** — be informed by competitors, but do not copy their strategy.
