# CRO Advisor

## When to Use
- Aligning sales and marketing for revenue growth
- Revenue strategy and target setting
- Go-to-market optimization
- Sales process and pipeline review
- Revenue leadership challenges

## Workflow

### Step 1: Revenue Assessment
- Current revenue and growth rate
- Revenue sources (channels, segments, products)
- Pipeline health (coverage ratio, stage conversion, velocity)
- Sales efficiency (CAC, sales cycle length, win rate)

### Step 2: Revenue Strategy
Define the revenue engine:
- **ICP**: Ideal customer profile (who to sell to)
- **GTM motion**: Product-led, sales-led, or hybrid
- **Pricing**: Aligned with value delivery
- **Channels**: Direct, partner, self-serve

### Step 3: Pipeline Management
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Pipeline coverage | Pipeline value / Quota | 3-4x |
| Stage conversion | Deals advancing / Deals entering stage | Depends on stage |
| Pipeline velocity | (Deals x Win Rate x Deal Size) / Sales Cycle | Increasing |
| Forecast accuracy | Actual / Forecast | >80% |

### Step 4: Sales-Marketing Alignment
- Shared definitions (MQL, SQL, opportunity)
- Shared targets (pipeline contribution by marketing)
- Regular pipeline review (weekly)
- Feedback loop (why did deals win/lose?)

### Step 5: Revenue Growth Levers
| Lever | Action |
|-------|--------|
| More leads | Improve marketing, expand channels |
| Better conversion | Improve sales process, enable sales team |
| Higher deal size | Upsell, cross-sell, move upmarket |
| Faster sales cycle | Remove friction, improve qualification |
| Less churn | Improve onboarding, customer success |

## Stack Context
- **Notion**: Revenue dashboards, pipeline tracking, and strategy docs.
- **Stripe**: Revenue data and subscription analytics.
- **Vercel Analytics**: Lead source and conversion tracking.

## Quality Gate
- [ ] Revenue assessment completed
- [ ] ICP and GTM motion defined
- [ ] Pipeline metrics tracked
- [ ] Sales-marketing alignment established
- [ ] Growth levers identified and prioritized

## Anti-Patterns
- **Revenue without retention** — acquiring customers who churn is an expensive treadmill.
- **Misaligned sales and marketing** — if they are not working toward shared metrics, they are working against each other.
- **No pipeline discipline** — deals that sit in the pipeline forever skew forecasts.
