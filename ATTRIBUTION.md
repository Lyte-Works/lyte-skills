## Deduplication

When multiple repositories offered the same capability, the winner was chosen by:
1. Most actionable (structured prompts with clear inputs/outputs)
2. Best structured (defined steps, checklists, deliverables)
3. Stack-aligned (compatible with Next.js/TypeScript/Tailwind/Vercel)
4. Most opinionated (clear stance over generic "it depends")
5. Narrowest scope (does one thing well)

See `skills-catalog.md` in the Lyte Workers Digital Agency repository for the complete deduplication analysis.
