# LinkedIn Authority Builder

## When to Use
- Building a founder or executive's LinkedIn presence
- LinkedIn is a key channel for the client's audience (B2B, SaaS, professional services)
- Establishing thought leadership
- LinkedIn profile gets views but no engagement

## Workflow

### Step 1: Profile Optimization
- **Headline**: Not job title. State the outcome you deliver. "Helping [audience] achieve [outcome]"
- **About**: First 3 lines are visible. Hook immediately. Then: what you do, who you help, proof, CTA
- **Banner image**: Reinforce positioning (not a generic stock photo)
- **Featured section**: Pin top-performing posts, case studies, or a lead magnet

### Step 2: Content Pillars (3-4)
Define what you will consistently post about:
1. Industry insights and trends
2. Behind-the-scenes / how we work
3. Lessons learned / failures and wins
4. Educational how-to content

### Step 3: Content Formats
| Format | Engagement | Effort | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Text post (story/insight) | High | Low | 3x/week |
| Carousel (slides) | Very High | Medium | 1x/week |
| Poll | Medium | Low | 1x/week |
| Article (long-form) | Medium | High | 1-2x/month |
| Video | High | High | Optional |
| Comment on others' posts | High | Low | Daily |

### Step 4: Post Structure
**Hook** (first 2 lines — visible before "see more"):
- Start with a bold statement, surprising stat, or contrarian take
- No hashtags in the hook. No "I'm excited to announce..."

**Body** (value):
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Line breaks between every paragraph
- Use numbered lists or bullet points
- Tell a story or provide a framework

**CTA** (engagement):
- Ask a question
- Invite a response
- Encourage sharing

### Step 5: Engagement Strategy
- **Comment first**: Spend 15 min/day commenting on others' posts before publishing your own
- **Reply to every comment** on your posts within 2 hours
- **Tag relevant people** (only if genuinely relevant)
- **Engage with your audience's posts** (build reciprocity)

### Step 6: Measure
Track monthly:
- Profile views
- Post impressions (average)
- Engagement rate
- Connection requests received
- Inbound leads from LinkedIn

## Quality Gate
- [ ] Profile optimized (headline, about, banner, featured)
- [ ] Content pillars defined (3-4)
- [ ] Publishing cadence set (minimum 3x/week)
- [ ] Engagement routine established (15 min/day)
- [ ] Monthly metrics tracked
- [ ] Content calendar maintained in Notion

## Anti-Patterns
- **Corporate speak** — LinkedIn rewards authenticity. Write like a person, not a press release.
- **Only self-promotion** — follow the 80/20 rule: 80% value, 20% promotion.
- **Inconsistency** — posting 5 times one week and zero the next kills the algorithm and your audience.
- **Engagement pods** — artificial engagement is detectable and damages credibility.
- **Ignoring comments** — not replying to comments signals you do not value your audience.
